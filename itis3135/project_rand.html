Assignment: A Overview

Jacob's Wonderful Web Development Work

Overview:

My current client sells cupcakes/bakery-related items. My client would like to have a website to display her products, favorite products, contact information, terms of service, and home page.

Intended Website Users:

Anyone who would like to purchase cupcakes, cakes, and other bakery delights.

Content Overview:

  1. Home - This will feature a menu to swap between each page on all pages. This page also explains more about my client.
  2. Products - Display photos of products and include their descriptions.
  3. Favorite Products - Displays a slideshow of clients' favorite products. This also displays photos of their favorite products and their product descriptions.
  4. Contact - Displays clients business contact information and their social media.
  5. Terms of Service - Displays clients terms of service for product purchases and inquiries.

Page Design:

1.) Page Names: Home, Product, Contact, Terms of Service, Angie's Favorites

2.) Purpose:

  1. Home - This will feature a menu to swap between each page on all pages. This page also explains more about my client.
  2. Product - Display photos of products and include their descriptions.
  3. Angie's Favorites - Displays a slideshow of clients' favorite products. This also displays photos of their favorite products and their product descriptions.
  4. Contact - Displays clients business contact information and their social media.
  5. Terms of Service - Displays clients terms of service for product purchases and inquiries.

3.) General Audience: Anyone who would prefer to purchase cupcakes, cakes, or any other bakery delights.

4.) Content: Described above in the purpose for each list item. Home contains a menu which links each webpage. Product displays product information and also contains the product generator. Similarly, the product generator function is also present on the favorites webpage. The contact webpage contains my clients contact information. The terms of service webpage displays my clients terms of service for product purchases and additional inquiries.

Enter Data: No. There will not be a page where users enter data.

Data Validation: Not needed. No data will be entered.

Buttons and Hyperlinks: Yes. The product and favorite products page will contain the button to randomly generate the item. The hyperlinks are present on each website in the header and footer menu.

Actions: On image hover, the image enlarges towards the user. The images have padding to ensure the images do not display above text.

Dynamic Functionality:

generateProduct(): When the user clicks the button, the button will generate a random product or favorite product. The product will be displayed on the screen.

  • This function will be present on the favorite products / angie's favorites page. It will also be present on the products page.
  • Example of implementation: Generate Random Number

imageSlideshow(): A constant image slideshow is present on the favorite products page. It cycles through the favorite products displayed on the favorite products page.

  • This function will be present on the favorite products / angie's favorites page.
  • Example of implementation: Image Slideshow Example

imagePreload(): Image Preload to load the images before the site is visible to the user. This is present on the whole site since my client has a logo or images on each page.

  • This function will be present on each page to account for any images / logos present on the page. The purpose of this function is to load the image before it is displayed. The main reason this is useful is so that the user does not see the box that contains the image while the page loads.
  • Example of implementation: Image Preload Example
